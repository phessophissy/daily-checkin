How It Works

Step 1: Connect

Click "Connect Wallet" and authorize the connection using your Stacks wallet (like Hiro Wallet or Xverse).

Step 2: Check In

Once connected, click the "Check In Today" button to submit your daily check-in transaction with a 0.001 STX fee.

Step 3: Earn

Your check-in is recorded on the blockchain. Maintain streaks to earn additional STX rewards!

Step 4: Track

View your statistics including total check-ins, current streak, and total STX earned in the dashboard.
